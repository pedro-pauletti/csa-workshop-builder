Claim Status

Look up the status of a claim by member ID and claim ID. The copilot always defers to the adjudication store as the source of truth — never paraphrases.

Look up a claim

Try NW-4471 / CLM-9803 (denied) or NW-7782 / CLM-9610 (in review).

Why this matters

  • Source of truth wins. The copilot reads from the adjudication store directly and surfaces the same numbers the back-office sees.
  • Denial explanations are templated. Reduces "agent re-interpretation" risk and gives compliance a paper trail.
  • Hand-off, not hand-wave. When status is "in review", the bot offers to open a callback ticket — no fabrication.
