## Business challenges

- Teams cannot quickly evaluate whether AI delivers trustworthy outcomes.
- Demos feel disconnected from real business requirements.
- Hard to scale a workshop pattern across customers and products.

## Workshop objective

Show how to design, demo and evaluate a grounded AI assistant pattern that can
be adapted to any customer's use case.

## Requirements

| ID | Requirement |
|----|-------------|
| R1 | Show a grounded chat assistant with citations. |
| R2 | Show a search demo with ranked results and scores. |
| R3 | Show a workflow demo with a step-by-step timeline. |
| R4 | Show a document analysis demo with structured extraction. |
| R5 | Show an evaluation dashboard with groundedness/relevance/harm scores. |

## Success criteria

- Audience can describe the grounded-answer architecture from memory.
- Architects identify two integration points for a pilot.
- The same skeleton is reused for the next customer with only content edits.

## Executive summary

A generic, agenda-driven workshop pattern that produces a runnable AI
assistant demo in hours and customizes to any customer in days.
