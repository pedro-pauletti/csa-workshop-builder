## Example — Contoso Telecom (Customer Service AI)

**Customer:** Contoso Telecom (fictional)
**Industry:** Telecommunications
**Region:** Brazil, LATAM
**Audience:** Customer service leaders, support operations, solution architects
**Microsoft product focus:** Azure AI Foundry, Azure AI Search, Azure OpenAI

**Business challenges**

- Hallucinations in customer-facing AI erode trust.
- Reps spend too long searching internal knowledge.
- Risk team requires evidence of safety and auditability.

**Workshop objective**

Show how grounded AI assistants can reduce call handling time by 20% while
preserving auditability.

**Requirements**

| ID | Requirement |
|----|-------------|
| R1 | Show grounded answers with citations from internal knowledge base. |
| R2 | Demonstrate agent-assisted next-best-action for support workflows. |
| R3 | Demonstrate handling-time reduction with side-by-side comparison. |
| R4 | Show evaluation: groundedness, relevance, harm scoring. |
| R5 | Show observability: traces, token usage, cost per resolved ticket. |

**Success criteria**

- Audience can describe the grounded-answer architecture from memory.
- Architects identify two concrete integration points for a pilot.
- Risk lead approves the safety + evaluation story.

**Executive summary**

Contoso wants a trusted, observable AI assistant for support agents that
reduces handling time without compromising compliance.
